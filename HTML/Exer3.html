     Rizal Park, also known as Luneta Park or simply Luneta, is a historical park located in Manila, Philippines. The park was named after the national hero of the Philippines,
    Jose Rizal, who was executed in 1896 by the Spanish colonial government for his role in the Philippine Revolution.

The Manila Baywalk is a promenade located along the Manila Bay in the Philippines. The baywalk, which stretches for several kilometers, offers a scenic view of the bay and the city's skyline. It was first built in 2007 as a joint project of the Manila city government and the private sector.

Intramuros is a historic walled city within the modern metropolis of Manila, Philippines. It was the seat of government and political power during the Spanish colonial period in the Philippines, from the 16th to the late 19th century.

The Philippines' National Museum of Fine Arts, also known as the National Art Gallery, is a museum located in Manila, Philippines. It is the oldest museum in the Philippines and one of the oldest in Southeast Asia.
